Banana Banana Bread

This is a nice looking banana bread recipe that I'm excited to try.

I will have to update this with more description and a review once I attempt this.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2 1/3 cups mashed overripe bananas
